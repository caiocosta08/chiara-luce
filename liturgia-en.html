Liturgy to celebrate Holy Mass on the day of Blessed Chiara Luce Badano - October 29

Prayer of the Day

Father of immense kindness, who through the merits of your Son and the gift of the Spirit made Blessing Chiara Badano burning with love, deeply transform our spirit so that we too, following his example, can always carry out with calm confidence your Holy will Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who is God, with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit.

1st Reading (At the choice of solemnity)

Ct 2, 10b-14

Responsorial Psalm:

Sal 15, 2; 4-11

Hold me, O God, for it is in you that I seek refuge.

In the solemnities: 2nd Reading:

1 Jn 4, 7-13

Acclamation to the Gospel

He that abideth in me, and I in him, bringeth forth much fruit.

Gospel: John 15, 1-11

Prayer over offerings

Bless the gifts we offer you, Lord, in remembrance of Blessed Chiara, and renew our spirit deeply, so that, free from the leaven of evil, let us live a new life in the light of the Gospel. Through Christ our Lord.

Prayer after Communion

May the communion on the table of the body and blood of your Son free us, Lord, from the seduction of the things that pass, and according to the example of Blessed Chiara help us to grow in your love, so that we may enjoy in heaven the vision of your face, Lord Jesus Christ your Son, who is God with you, in the unity of the Holy Spirit. Amen.
